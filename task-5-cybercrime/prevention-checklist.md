## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown payment links or messages  
- Always verify the sender before accepting payment requests  
- Do not scan random QR codes from untrusted sources  
- Use official and trusted apps for transactions  
- Enable app lock and two-factor authentication  
- Regularly check your bank statements for suspicious activity  
- Avoid sharing personal or financial details online  
- Immediately report fraud on https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud
