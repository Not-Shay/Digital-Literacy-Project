## Social Media Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do's
- Be respectful while communicating online  
- Think before posting any content  
- Keep your profile professional  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Protect your personal information  

### ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share passwords or sensitive data  
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful content  
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation  
- Avoid oversharing personal details  
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
